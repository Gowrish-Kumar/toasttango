ABOUT US

The TOAST TANGO Cuisine

The perfect place to enjoy the delicious food

We are proud to have our restaurant's history being as long as 25 years! Since the day we opened our doors for the first time in 1991, we served tens of thousands of people, sharing our love for a traditionally Indian cuisine with the local citizens, as well as with all guests of our city…

Coming to us for a dinner should feel just as comfortable, as having one at home. The simplicity, freshness and reasonable pricing for our dishes just underlines how home-like our restaurant is…

ENJOY THE TASTE OF THE CHARCOAL GRILLING PERFECTED BY YEARS OF EXPERIENCE

The Toast Tango Cuisine is the result of over 25 years of Classic Outdoor Grilling - from the people who invented the Charcoal kettle grill. You will taste the difference that decades of grilling expertise make.Our secret is easy and perfect at the same time. We are committed to using quality ingredients, innovative recipes, honed grilling technique, and grill charcoal kettles. There is a little something for everyone on our menu, however, we are especially proud of our Award-Winning Burgers, Slow-Smoked Regional BBQ, Hand-Cut Black Angus Steaks and Grilled Seafood items.
